The Past Trajectory of the Environment



Have you ever stopped to think about how the environment was during the origins of the Earth in comparison to how it is today? It has indeed changed to a great extent. The Earth has continued its cycle for several years, however, after several humans and species began to diversify on this planet, experts say Carbon Dioxide levels have increased to a great extent and will continue to do so in the near future. This comes from fossil fuel emissions, reduction of natural resources, and more demand than supply.



The Future Trajectory of the Environment


The trajectory of the evironment right now is currently headed to warmer atmospheres, a warmer and more acidic ocean, higher sea levels due to ice melting at a faster rate, larger changes in percipitation patterns, the shirking of ice sheets and ice caps at a faster rate, and more intense and frequent heat waves.



Solutions to Climate Change


Some solutions include lowering the usage of energy in your home, replace methods of transportation that emit greehouse gases, such as cars, buses and motorcycles, with transportation methods such as walking and bicycling, cut down on less red meat consumption, litter less, recycle, reuse and repair more and maybe consider switching to an electric vehicle.
