Information about the Environment



What is Climate Change


Climate Change is shifts in both temperature and weather patterns due to the that occur over long intervals of time.

Causes of Environmental Changes


Some causes of Climate Change include:
  • - The generation of power through the use of fossil fuels
  • - The cutting down of trees and forests
  • - Emission of Greenhouse Gases
  • - Volcanic Activity
  • - Consumer Production/Manufacturing of goods
  • - Reflection and Absorbtion of the Suns Energy


Environmental Impacts of Climate Change


  • - Biodiversity Loss (Extinction of different species)
  • - Increased Natural Disaters
  • - Scarcity of Water
  • - Increased Famines
  • - Loss of dry land (due to increasing sea levels)
  • - Poverty and Displacement
  • - Hotter Temperatures
  • - frequent Heat Waves
